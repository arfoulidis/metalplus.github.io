Company profile

Our company has been operating in the frames industry since 2002. With constantly growing productivity and zeal for the job, we managed to rank our company in one of the brand names in the sector, with sales points throughout Greece and abroad.

With stable and decisive steps and customer driven only, we managed to develop METAL PLUS against nowadays economic crisis. METAL PLUS bases its growth in values such as the assimilation of its executives, total customer satisfaction, quality production based on know-how and the use of certified materials. Our company is certified according to ISO 9001, as our products are produced with the strictest specifications and with modern equipment.

History


METAL PLUS was founded in 2002 by Dimitrios Konstantinidis, based in Neochori, Prefecture of Serres. In 2008, the management of METAL PLUS decides to expand its facilities and equip its production unit with modern machines, producing window systems with the strictest specifications. In the same year, the company is certified with ISO 9001: 2008 and launches a new exhibition space, thus enabling its customers to get to know the full range of products.

In 2012, having achieved our target in the local market, we decide to expand our vision and with the most important allies, the company's human resources and quality products, we create a sales network in the Greek and island islands, as well as abroad.

  • 2016 - Participation in the 2nd International Business Exhibition of Serres SER-EXPO 2016
  • 2012 - Creating a network of partners in Greece and abroad
  • 2008 - ISO Certificate 9001 : 2008
  • 2008 - Opening of our Exhibition
  • 2008 - Inauguration of the production facility
  • 2002 - Foundation of the company METAL PLUS by Dimitris Konstantinidis.
