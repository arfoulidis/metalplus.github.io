Frame 14 point ECO

Armored Door


Description

14-point ECO frame offers security even with an unlocked door.

  • 2 steel galvanized vertical ribs 0,50mm from the inside part of the door.
  • Steel sheet 0,50mm thick on the outside part of the door.
  • Steel case with material thickness 10/25mm.
  • Easy placement of fittings in comparison with “Camera Europea” systems.
  • High level of insulation thanks to the use of EPDM sealing gaskets.
  • Water tightness: 900 Pa in single sash with fixed sub-light (13 Beaufort cyclone).
  • Internal joint corner with set up holes for the hinges for optimal stabilization.
  • Available in linear and curvy shapes.
  • All types of constructions.
  • Part of ELVIAL's European License.
